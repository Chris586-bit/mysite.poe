ABOUT LOGITECH

LogiTech is on a mission to be a conduit between prospective students who are ambitious and the opportunities that lie ahead in the world of IT. We look to empower learners and students from all backgrounds — but disadvantaged ones in particular — to master the technology skills that are in demand through high-quality and flexible online education.

Founded by an honors computer science student who is now an IT professional, the platform offers a dynamic mix of courses that are self-paced, training, and hands-on projects designed to equip our students for real-world challenges. The platform provides tools, mentorship, and accredited certifications for your success in the world of IT. We offer various coding courses including Introduction to Full Stack Development, Introduction to Programming, Basic to Advanced Java, Database Design and Development, and Front-end Technologies.

MISSION

To establish and provide affordable and sustainable IT teaching services that will meet the needs and standards of our students.

VISION

To be a pioneer within the IT industry on a global spectrum and to be roundly known for quality, excellence, and value-added projects that will empower our communities at large.
