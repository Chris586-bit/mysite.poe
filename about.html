ABOUT LOGITECH

LogiTech is a on mission to be a conduit between prospective students who are ambitious and the opprportunities that lie ahead in the world of IT. We look to empower learners and students from all backgrounds but disadvantaged ones in particular, to master the technology skills that are in demand through high-quality and flexible online education.

Founded by an honors computer science student who is now an IT professional, the platform offers a dynamic mix of courses that are self-paced, training and hands-on projects that are designed to equip our students and prepare them for real-world challenges. The platform provides tools, mentorship and accredited certifications for your success in the world of IT. We offer various coding that includes introduction to full stack development, introduction to programming, Basic Java to Advanced Java, Database design and development, and Front-end technologies.
